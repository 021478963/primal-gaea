What is Primal Gaea?

Primal Gaea starts off from a 2D browser game inspired by RPG games found in the early 2000s. It will then evolve into a game with its own client that is a full-fledge 3D MMORPG with its own metaverse, lore and backstory. Playersâ€™ actions will alter the course of the world and will compete not only by combat but with politics and economics. The base game will always be there, but the metaverse will allow players living in Primal Gaea to create their own businesses, real estate matters and strategy will be the key to success.

Unique Combat Scenario System

Powerful weapons, armor, potions don't come easily. Those who are tenacious enough to keep trying even though things get difficult are rewarded with unimaginable riches as discovering new powerful inventions will not only serve them but the greater good of the community.

Inventory System

Our combat system takes the best of preparedness, RNG and variety. Testing the players ability art of preparation, decisions making skill and if lady luck is on your side

Progressive Gameplay Actual Play to Earn Model

Primal Gaea economy is its unique selling point, everything in Primal Gaea is considered and thought of, every NFT or FT has its use and purpose, nothing is minted or produced just for the sake of it, a fine balance between utility and collection but alas it will not be perfect, however, protocols and adjustments will be made reactively and effectively if problems arise. Community feedback will be welcome and considered. After all this Primal Gaea is run by the inhabitants.
